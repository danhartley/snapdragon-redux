About Snapdragon

Snapdragon is a tool for studying the 1.3 million recorded species on Earth.

Related species are grouped and tested in lessons.

Lessons take the form of concise summaries followed by quick tests.

If you have any queries or would like to propose a lesson please contact me.

This site will change, and may break (it is a functional prototype that is updated regularly). If you are having problems click on the at the bottom of the screen and delete the cache. This will reload the page (but also delete your score).

Good luck!
