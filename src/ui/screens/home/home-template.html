Welcome!
Learn The Planet is a collection of a video lessons about the natural world, from how to forage for mushrooms, to growing herbs indoors.
Each video is supplemented by notes, navigation and a lesson review.
Tapping on the home icon below, will take you to the main menu. Once there, tap on a icon to begin a lesson.
Click on a lesson to watch a video, or on the to view a lesson species guide.
If you are interested in wildlife near you, or in specific species, click on the above the lesson list and follow the guide to create your own lesson.
These lessons took time and effort to make; Learn The Planet would like to thank the people who created them.
