Welcome to Snapdragon.

Snapdragon improves your ability to recognise and name species.

The casual observer will learn about the trees, plants and animals they see around them every day.

Dedicated naturalists have the opportunity to study the features that are common to species and that link them.

Snapdragon is all about learning and the best way to do that is to put new found knowledge to the test.

Snapdragon tests are based on collections. Pick the collection that interests you and start learning. The tests increase in difficulty as you proceed.

For example Quercus suber, the Cork Oak, is part of the Mediterranean 1 collection. Your abililty to recognise this tree and identify its common and scientific names will be tested first. The final test requires you to enter the full scientific - or latin - name so pay attention along the way!

To see details of a specimen click on an image. Depending on the size of your screen you will see links that display additional information or take you to other sites such as Wikipedia.

Good luck!
